The Short Version

A downtown fixture, not a concept.

Frank's has been folding slices in the Historic District long enough that "new" employees have been here a decade. The dough's thin, the cheese is generous, and the upstairs seating fills up fast once the after-work crowd figures out it's Tuesday again.

Regulars order without looking at the board. First-timers usually leave saying some version of the same thing: reasonably priced, genuinely clean, and the hardest-working crew on Travis Street.
